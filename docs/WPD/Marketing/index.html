WPD/Marketing

Promoting News and Events

  1. Write a compelling blog article on WebPlatform.org

    • Get feedback and approval on the post from the community and stewards on public-webplatform (see blog posting guidelines)

  2. Tweet a link from @webplatform (~6k followers), using a Piwik tracking URL

  3. Retweet from @w3c (~60k followers) and other Team accounts and partners (e.g. members, stewards, etc.)

  4. Retweet again in a different timezone cycle (or make a follow-up tweet

  5. Engage people who ask questions or make comments on Twitter

  6. Post to social media sites, such as Google+ and Facebook
