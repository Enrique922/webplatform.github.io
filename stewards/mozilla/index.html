Mozilla

Mozilla operates with a mission to promote openness, innovation and opportunity on the Internet. We're a proudly non-profit organization dedicated to keeping the power of the Web in people's hands. We're also a global community of users, contributors and developers working to innovate on the behalf of users everywhere. Anyone who uses a Mozilla product becomes a part of that community, helping us build a brighter future for the Web.

We are excited about the Web Platform Docs project because it aligns with Mozilla’s mission to promote openness and innovation on the web. By involving the broader web community as well as organizations, we can help make sure more developers have access to up-to-date, comprehensive and practical information.

Stormy Peters, Director of Websites and Developer Engagement, Mozilla
